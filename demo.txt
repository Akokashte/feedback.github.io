Steps for Execution of website.

step-1) Open terminal or powershell.
step-2) Terminal/powershell should contain path of the project folder.
step-3) Execute command -> python manage.py runserver.
step-4) Paste this url in your browser -> http://127.0.0.1:8000/.
step-5) Signup and login with valid credentials.
step-6) Then click on 'click here' hyperlink to open feedback form.
step-7) Fill feedback form and submit.
step-8) If you login as a admin then admin tab will appear in navbar.
step-9) Click on Admin
step-10) Visualization of ratings will be displayed.
